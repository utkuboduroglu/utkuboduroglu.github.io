Hello there! Thanks for checking out my blog! This is sort of an
update on a digital space that I've had for a while but desperately
needed to revamp. There have been a couple iterations of this page by
now, ranging from one made hastily with a Jekyll template, to one I've
tried to program by hand to practice JS, to this one right now; one
that's a balance between a DIY project, and one that's also situated
on top of a mature piece of software (again, Jekyll).

These pages will still be changing rapidly for the first couple of
months this entry has been live, because I've found that waiting for
the blog to _resemble a final product before deploying it and before
writing entries for it_ makes the process effectively impossible. As a
result, the entirety of this blog is a **work in progress**. With that
being said, there are concrete reasons why it exists in the first
place.
